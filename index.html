Expert Legal Grounds

Your Debt is Civil, Not Criminal.

Most borrowers believe that defaulting on a personal loan or credit card is a crime that can lead to immediate arrest. This is incorrect. Debt default is a civil matter.

  • RBI Circular DBOD.No.BP.BC.121/21.04.048/2008-09 strictly prohibits any form of intimidation or harassment.
  • Right to Privacy: Banks cannot share your details with anyone except as permitted by law.
  • Legal Representation: You have the right to have a lawyer handle the negotiation for you.
