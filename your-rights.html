Know Your Legal Rights

You are protected by Indian Law and RBI Guidelines.

RBI Fair Practices Code

The Reserve Bank of India has laid down strict guidelines for recovery agents appointed by banks and NBFCs. Violation of these guidelines is a punishable offense.

  • ‚úì
    No Threats or Abuse (IPC 503 & 506)

    Recovery agents strictly cannot use abusive language, shout, or threaten physical harm to you or your family.

  • ‚úì
    Restricted Calling Hours

    Calls are only permitted between 8:00 AM and 7:00 PM. Any call outside these hours is a violation of the RBI Fair Practices Code.

  • ‚úì
    Right to Privacy

    Agents cannot harass your family members, friends, or colleagues. They cannot visit your workplace to create a scene.

  • ‚úì
    No Public Shaming (Defamation IPC 499)

    It is illegal to post your photos or details on social media, or send messages to your contact list shaming you for non-payment.

  • ‚úì
    Identity Verification

    Recovery agents must carry an authorization letter from the bank and an ID card. You have the right to refuse to talk to them if they don't produce these.
